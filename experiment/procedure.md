### Procedure

### Step 1: Weighing of Cement
•   Click on the weighing plate to place it on the weighing machine.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•    Click on tare to set the reading to zero.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/1.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Using a spatula, accurately weigh 200 g of cement.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/1.3.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on Next to proceed.

### Step 2: Weighing of Water
•   Click on the beaker to place it on the weighing machine.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/2.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the Tare button to measure its weight.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/2.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the measuring cylinder and weigh water equal to 0.78P × weight of cement.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/2.3.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   After measuring the required quantity of water, click on Next.

### Step 3: Preparation of Cement Paste
•   Click on the beaker to add water to the cement.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/3.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the spatula and mix thoroughly to obtain a uniform and homogeneous paste.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/3.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   After proper mixing, click on Next.

### Step 4: Filling the Le Chatelier’s Mould
•   Click on the brush and apply a thin layer of oil to the inner surface of the mould.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the glass plate and place the mould apparatus on it.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br> 
•   Using a spatula, fill the mould completely with the prepared cement paste.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.3.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the apparatus to spread the paste evenly.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.4.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Place another glass plate on top of the mould.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.5.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>  
•   Click on the weight and place it over the glass plate to apply uniform pressure.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/4.6.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on Next.

### Step 5: Immersion in Water
•   Click on the Le Chatelier’s apparatus to immerse the complete assembly in the water tank.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/5.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   After 24 hours, click on the apparatus to remove it from the water.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/5.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   Click on the scale to measure the distance between the two indicator points of the mould.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/5.3.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br> 
•   Click on Next.

### Step 6: Boiling and Final Measurement
•   Click on the setup to place the mould assembly into boiling water.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/6.1.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
•   After boiling, click on the scale and measure the distance between the two indicator points again.<br>
<img src="procedure_images/6.2.png" alt="procedure" width="500"><br>
 
Finally, click on Post Test to evaluate your understanding of the experiment.
